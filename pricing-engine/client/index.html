Price Table Builder

How this works:
  • Pick a product and a size. Generate produces a price table for every variant combo (paper stock, folding, thickness, etc.) at that size, across all quantity break points and allowed finishings.
  • Markup % is applied last — after base cost + add-ons + finishing + turnaround multiplier.
  • Quantities not in the price table are linearly interpolated between break points; below the smallest / above the largest they clamp to that endpoint.
  • One column per allowed turnaround appears in the output. Restrict turnarounds in the Products tab if a product doesn't offer all of them.

Prices

Edit price tables here.
  • Pick a product. All sizes are shown at once, grouped by size.
  • To add a new size, paper stock, or quantity break point, go to the Products tab — those are structural settings.
  • Empty cells are allowed. The engine interpolates between filled break points and clamps outside the range.
